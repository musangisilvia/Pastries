Sale! Sale! Sale!

Think of a yard sale but instead of furniture and things you do not need there's pastries and more pastries!
In addition to pastries made and sold at the shop, customers as well as other interested parties are allocated stands where they sell their pastries or any other confectionaries.

Customer Appreciation

Our customers are truly our biggest supporters and the reason we open shop everyday.
Because of this, we organize an event every once in a while where we let all our customers,
Young and old to come to the shop and witness how we make some of our recepies as well as
make suggestions of how we can improve in our service and ofcourse pastries.

Children in the Kitchen

Every year, we set aside a day where we organise an event for children between the age of 8 and 15. On this day,we select some of our recepies and guide the children through the baking process.
